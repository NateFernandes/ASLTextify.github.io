Privacy Policy

Welcome to ASLTextify. Protecting your privacy is our priority, and this policy details how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our ASL translation services.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Personal Information

To enhance your experience, we may ask for basic information when you create an account. This includes:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted form)

1.2 Usage Data

We collect non-personal data regarding how users interact with our services to improve overall performance. This includes:

  • Frequency of Visits: Information on how often users access the ASL-to-text and text-to-ASL features.
  • Interaction Patterns: How users engage with different sections, including session length and specific pages viewed.
  • Device and Browser Type: Technical details such as the type of device and browser you use, which helps us optimize the service for different platforms.

This data is aggregated and used solely for the purpose of enhancing user experience.

1.3 Camera and Sign Data (for ASL-to-Text Translation)

Camera Access: The ASL-to-Text translation feature uses real-time camera input. This data is only processed temporarily during usage and is not stored on our servers.

Sign Recognition: The signs captured are processed directly within the app to convert them into text. No raw video data is saved.

Note: You will be prompted to allow camera access every session. We strictly adhere to permissions to ensure your data is only processed with your consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: Providing ASL-to-Text and Text-to-ASL translations.
  • Account Management: Managing your profile, handling password resets, and sending essential notifications.
  • Improvements: Analyzing usage data to improve our system accuracy and user experience.
  • Security: Protecting against unauthorized access and ensuring secure usage.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may disclose data in the following cases:

  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or a government request.
  • Service Improvements: With trusted partners to analyze and improve system performance, who are obligated to uphold strict data confidentiality.

4. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Encryption: We use encryption protocols for data storage and transfer.
  • Secure Access: Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Security Audits: We routinely assess our systems for vulnerabilities to maintain data security.

5. User Control & Data Management

5.1 Accessing & Updating Information

You can view and edit your personal data via your profile settings.

5.2 Camera Permissions

Each session will prompt you to confirm camera access, giving you full control over when and how your camera is used.

5.3 Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services. Data not required for active use is securely deleted after [specified time period].

5.4 Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account from the settings. Deleting your account will erase all personal data within [time frame]. After deletion, some usage data may remain in aggregate form for system improvements.

6. Updates to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect service or legal changes. We encourage you to review it periodically.

7. Contact Us

For questions or concerns, please visit our Contact Us page.
