About Us

At ASLTextify, we are dedicated to bridging the communication gap for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. Our innovative web-based application leverages cutting-edge machine learning and computer vision technologies to provide real-time translation between American Sign Language (ASL) and text or voice. We strive to create an inclusive environment where communication is seamless and accessible for everyone, enhancing interactions in educational, professional, and social settings.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the deaf and hard-of-hearing community by providing a reliable and user-friendly translation tool. We believe that effective communication is a fundamental right, and our goal is to make ASLTextify a cornerstone in achieving digital accessibility and inclusion.

Our Technology

We utilize state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms and advanced computer vision techniques to ensure high accuracy and real-time processing. Our project showcases the potential of these technologies to facilitate effective communication for ASL users and those who do not understand ASL.

Our Commitment

At ASLTextify, we are committed to delivering a robust and functional application that meets the needs of our users. We have meticulously designed and tested our features to ensure usability and reliability, providing a tool that supports seamless communication in various settings.
