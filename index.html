WHO WE ARE

Our Journey to Accessibility

ASLTextify began as a university project, driven by a mission to create an inclusive tool that translates American Sign Language (ASL) into text. As a group of students, we set out to solve a real-world problem, and now we're on the path to bridging communication gaps between the Deaf and hearing communities.

Learn More

Driven by Purpose, Powered by Innovation

We believe that technology can empower individuals and bring people closer. ASLTextify aims to revolutionize the way ASL is understood by providing a real-time translation platform that allows anyone to communicate freely. Our goal is to make conversations accessible to all, regardless of language barriers.
